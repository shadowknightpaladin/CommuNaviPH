What is CommuNaviPH?

SakayGuide is your one stop terminal for all your commuting knowledge needs! We feature helpful, illustrated, step-by-step guides for you to follow before you venture out into the Philippine commuting landscape. Each guide features quick tips and tricks to help you move like a veteran.

In each guide is a tip to keep you and your valuables safe! We also have a Commuting Etiquette guide to keep your commute nice and peaceful!

History

CommuNaviPH's first inception was a guide known plainly as COMMUNAVI. It was a final project requirement for a Graphic Design class. The original guide featured only the main steps and basic illustrations.

The next iteration came in the re-modelled form of SakayGuide (not yet launched as of writing). This remodelled the site to fit in with Sakay's branding. This refined the illustrations used, added new content such as the safety tips, walking guide and the alteration of the homepage.

The current iteration of CommuNaviPH had the most changes. It now includes a remastered homepage, illustrations, colors and layout. It also now includes the guides for Commuting Etiquette, an MRT/LRT map and a new PDF Downlaodable version of the guides. It also now includes the CommuCards that can be found both on the facebook page and around the metro.
